MUAY THAI

This combat sport originated in Thailand in the 1500's. "The art of 8 limbs" utilizes fists, feet, elbows, and knees as striking weapons, in addition to clinch and sweep/throw techniques.

MIXED MARTIAL ARTS

Incorporating strikes from boxing and Muay Thai with grappling techniques from wrestling and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. Learn takedowns, submissions, ground defense and escapes.
